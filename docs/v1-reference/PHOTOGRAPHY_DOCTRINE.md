# Gangatirtham — Photography Doctrine

> The visual companion to `CONTENT_DOCTRINE.md` and `DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`. Governs all
> placeholder and archive imagery. **Documentary evidence of a real river**, graded into
> the Tirtha temperature ramp, organised by the 8 stages of the river's life. Warmth comes
> from **light, material, and the human hand — never hue, ornament, glow, or iconography.**
> If a frame could sit in a tourism brochure, it is wrong by construction.

## Note on the book vs the website (a deliberate divergence)

The book being sold — **"Ganga Tiram · Gomukh to Gangasagar" by Rasacharya Das** ("75 Panoramic
Locations") — is **devotional–panoramic**: saturated composite montages, a glowing-gold title,
the Jagannath deities, the Ganga-aarti spectacle. The website doctrine is the **opposite
register** — documentary, austere, desaturated, the periphery. This is intentional: the website
is the **museum-grade elevation** of a popular devotional book, per the locked brand ("must NOT
feel religious-propaganda / tourism"). The site's imagery is meant to diverge from the book by
design. *(Open item: the site's book metadata says "Gangatirtham Vol I"; reconcile with the real
title/author.)*

## The fixed grade (all imagery)

warm-desaturated **25–40% chroma** · **bone/ink-mapped** (never `#FFF`/`#000`) · film grain **3–6%**
· **4:5** primary (3:2 secondary) · **eye-level** · **available light** · an **act, not a pose** ·
the worn, the human, the periphery. **LIVE** = warm documentary encounter (colour). **ARCHIVE** =
monochrome deep-time record. Every image carries the provenance & ethics ledger (source · license ·
subject · consent · mode · km).

## The stage-by-stage visual system

Each stage inherits its **temperature** from km (the ramp) and its **mood** from `lib/journey.ts`.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| I | **Birth** · glacial source · glacial | austere wonder, shadowed by mortality | 35–50mm · wide→medium, human dwarfed | rare, peripheral — the watch on the origin (a glaciologist measuring the retreat) | coolest, ~30% chroma; highest archival (deep-time) |
| II | **Naming** · mountain confluence · glacial | quiet recognition | 50mm · medium, the seam / a hand on a manuscript | a named keeper of lineage/language (Art) | glacial, the water-seam the only note; Archive-leaning |
| III | **Testing** · ghat town · meltwater | clear-eyed love (worship & use in one gesture) | 50–85mm · close on hands; festival at the edge | the labour behind the rite (the sweeper, the wick-folder) | first warmth, muted; Live opens |

| V | **Reckoning** · the light-city · silt→warm | grave peace (dying is the point) | 50mm · medium-close; the shroud, never the pyre | the witness who sits with the dying | warming, never golden-hour; Live opens, Archive deepens |
| VI | **Working Life** · working river town · sandbar | unworshipped dignity | 50–85mm · close on the maker's hand, process | the maker, the boatman — unworshipped labour | sandbar warmth; Live (Craft) |

stage-7 *interruption* (the cut); Stage VIII echoes its *mending/opening* (the horizon widens).

## FACE doctrine (subordinate to the river)

FACE imagery **inherits the stage's grade and temperature** — a FACE entity at Stage V is shot in
the Reckoning palette. The wing is the subject's *type*; the stage is its *mood*. FACE never gets
its own visual identity.
- **Festival** — preparation & aftermath, the periphery (the wick-folder, the sweeper). Never the peak/drone/crowd-stock.
- **Art** — the living culture kept hand-to-hand (a hand on the tabla, a manuscript). Often Archive-monochrome; never a staged performance.
- **Craft** — the maker's hand, process over product. Live, warm; the object is by-product.
- **Environment** — stewardship/continuity (the steward measuring; loss with a real number). Gravity, never shock. At Stage VII only, the obituary register (Archive).

## Sankalp doctrine (keeping a lamp, not donation imagery)

The lamp is **inscriptional, never a glow**. Sankalp imagery, when it exists, is **the lamp as
object / the keeping as act** — a hand folding a wick, a worn lamp on the liberation-house register,
the *documentation being made* (the witness interview, the consent form, the plate graded), framed
by the stage the lamp sustains. **Never** donation imagery: no smiling beneficiaries, hands-receiving,
before/after, children-in-need, "your gift helped," or glowing-diya-on-water.

## Generation rules (prompt discipline)

**ONLY:** documentary still · available light · eye-level · 4:5 (or 3:2) · warm-desaturated 25–40%
chroma · bone/ink-mapped · film grain 3–6% · the worn / the human / the periphery · an act, not a pose.

**NEVER (negative-prompt every generation):** fantasy / illustration / concept-art / 3D-render /
painterly · glow / bloom / halo / god-rays / lens-flare / "spiritual light" · cinematic teal-orange /
heavy LUT / anamorphic · HDR / oversaturated / travel-brochure · golden-hour-with-a-holy-man (the
Incredible-India template) · drone / aerial spectacle · the postcard peak (aarti, pyre, **levitating
diya**) · NGO shock / poverty-tourism · text / watermark / logo / caption in the frame · pure `#FFF`/`#000`.

**Hard rule (journalistic integrity):** a documentary archive of a *real* river. **AI imagery may
never be published as evidence of a real place, person, or event.** Synthetic frames are pre-shoot
placeholders only, always carry `license: "AI-placeholder — not for publication"`, and are replaced
before a place reaches standard/flagship depth.

## Asset roadmap

- **Tier 0 — the gradient (now, everywhere):** the `.plate-*` km-graded plate is the honest "forthcoming" state. Ships gracefully.
- **Tier 1 — AI placeholder ACCEPTABLE:** graded, **non-identifiable, non-claim** atmosphere/texture — stub & standard heroes, FACE/Library/collection thumbnails. Carries the placeholder ledger flag. Temporary and labelled.

## Stage Visual Validation Set (one master frame per stage)

Stage archetypes, not named places — to validate the visual *language* before rollout.

### Stage I · Birth
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 35mm, deep focus. A thin braid of grey meltwater emerging from the dark mouth of a high-altitude Himalayan glacier; a single small figure in worn outdoor clothing crouches at the water's edge taking a measurement, dwarfed by ice and scree. Flat overcast altitude light, thin mist. Vast negative space, glacier mouth off-centre, high horizon. Muted warm-desaturated grade, cool blue-grey cast, ~30% saturation, bone-and-ink, fine film grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** drone, aerial, vista, postcard, golden hour, saturated color, HDR, glow, bloom, god-rays, lens flare, holy man, sadhu, ritual, flags, tourists, text, watermark, illustration, 3D render, painterly, teal-orange, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** a beginning that already knows its end — measuring the glacier's retreat is the mortality; the dwarfed human is smallness before deep time.
- **Avoids:** a worn measurement, not a scenic peak; no holy-man golden-hour; cool desaturation forecloses HDR tourism.

### Stage II · Naming
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 50mm, natural perspective. The meeting of two mountain rivers, a visible colour-seam between paler and darker water; foreground, a weathered hand resting on an old handwritten manuscript at the river's edge. Clear cold mountain daylight. Restrained composition, the seam a structural diagonal. Muted warm-desaturated grade, cool tonality, low saturation, bone-and-ink, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** temple spectacle, idols, incense smoke, glow, golden hour, drone, HDR, saturated color, fantasy, illustration, text, watermark, lens flare, holy man, painterly, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** the confluence where she is named — the hand on the manuscript is lineage and language; quiet recognition.
- **Avoids:** documentary seam, not a confluence-vista; no devotional/temple spectacle.

### Stage III · Testing
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 85mm, medium focus. Close on working hands cupping river water at a worn stone ghat step in early morning; behind, out of focus, the *edge* of a gathering — a sweeper's broom, a folded cloth — the labour around a ritual, never its centre. Soft available morning light. Muted warm-desaturated grade, low saturation, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** aarti, flames, diya on water, crowd spectacle, priests performing, drone, golden hour, HDR, saturated color, glow, text, watermark, fantasy, illustration, painterly, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** worship and use in the same gesture — the first hands; the periphery (Festivals = preparation/aftermath).
- **Avoids:** explicitly *not* the aarti — the banned Incredible-India frame.

### Stage IV · Gathering
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 50mm. A single elderly woman pilgrim at the waterline of a vast confluence at dawn, close and frontal; an immense crowd reduced to a soft indistinct grey band far behind, unreadable as individuals. Flat dusty plains light. She is the only sharp subject. Muted warm-desaturated grade, neutral silt-grey tonality, low saturation, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** drone, aerial crowd, Kumbh spectacle, mass-of-people hero, tents, saturated festival color, HDR, golden hour, glow, text, watermark, illustration, painterly, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** the largest crowd met as one face — the multitude is context, the one human the subject.
- **Avoids:** the deliberate refusal of the drone/crowd-spectacle.

### Stage V · Reckoning
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 50mm, frontal. A freshly laundered white shroud drying on a line above worn stone steps at a riverside at first light; mid-ground, an older woman in a plain sari sits quietly, a caretaker, hands folded; the river wide and flat behind. Soft warm morning available light, restrained. Muted warm-desaturated grade, low saturation, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** burning pyre, fire, cremation flames, smoke, skulls, sadhu, graphic death, golden hour glow, NGO grief, HDR, saturated color, drone, text, watermark, illustration, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** dying is the point, held without breaking — the flame, never the pyre; the witness who sits with the dying.
- **Avoids:** not the burning-ghat spectacle; grief earned through restraint.

### Stage VI · Working Life
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 85mm. Close on the hands of a weaver at a wooden handloom in a dim riverside workshop, threads under tension, mid-motion; ordinary working daylight from an open door; the process fills the frame, the finished cloth unseen. Muted warm-desaturated grade, sandbar-warm tonality, low saturation, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** ritual, worship, festival, glamour, staged craft display, product beauty-shot, golden hour, HDR, saturated color, glow, drone, text, watermark, illustration, painterly, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** she rises and simply works, unworshipped — the maker's hand, process over product.
- **Avoids:** no ritual frame; process, not a craft beauty-shot.

### Stage VII · Wound
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 50mm, restrained. The hard concrete geometry of a river-barrage gate cutting across a thinned, sluggish river under flat grey light; foreground, a fisherman crouched beside a near-empty net, or a handwritten dolphin-survey tally on a clipboard; the river visibly diminished. Near-monochrome, the warm grade drained to lowest saturation, bone-and-ink, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** dead-fish hero, graphic pollution, garbage close-up, crying faces, NGO guilt diptych, protest signs, saturated warning colours, HDR, drone, glow, text, watermark, illustration, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** the one wound she does not deserve — the chain (barrage), the thinning, a real number; the drained colour *is* the river thinning; the sanctioned obituary (karuna = one beat).
- **Avoids:** not NGO shock / dead-fish hero — gravity through restraint and specificity.

### Stage VIII · Return
- **Prompt:** Documentary photograph, eye-level, 28mm wide, deep focus, low wide view. A great river losing its banks and merging into the open sea across a flat delta of mudflats and distant mangroves at dawn; a single small figure stands at the water's threshold facing the opening horizon. Soft warm delta light, horizon low and wide. Muted warm-desaturated grade, delta-amber warmth but restrained low saturation, bone-and-ink, fine grain. 4:5.
- **Negative:** saturated sunset, orange sky, golden-hour cliché, glowing sun, lens flare, drone, aerial, HDR, postcard beach, boats-for-scenery, text, watermark, illustration, painterly, #fff, #000.
- **Represents:** she widens past being a river at all, given back to sea and sky — the opening horizon; release, a witness at the threshold.
- **Avoids:** not a saturated sunset/beach-postcard; restraint keeps the warm pole from tipping into HDR.
